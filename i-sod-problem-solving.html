Part III — Problem-Solving Rubric

Grade 12 Physics · Final Exam 2025–2026 · Problem Solving (50 pts) + Bonus (3 pts). g = 10 m/s². Represent weight as Fg. Right / up = positive. Scoring = method + answer split.

⚖ PARTIAL CREDIT: award the method marks even if the final number is wrong. Green badges = where each point goes.

Q1 · Acceleration of a pushed block (8 pts)
Given: m = 4 kg, Fnet = 12 N, frictionless. Formula: Fnet = m · a
Identify: m = 4 kg, Fnet = 12 N → rearrange a = Fnet / m
a = 12 / 4 = 3 m/s²
Identify m & Fnet = 2 pts Rearrange a = Fnet/m = 2 pts Substitute 12 / 4 = 2 pts a = 3 m/s² = 2 pts
Q2 · Weight and normal force (8 pts)
Given: m = 8 kg, g = 10 m/s², flat floor, no extra vertical force. Formula: Fg = m · g ; Fn = Fg
Fg = m · g = 8 × 10 = 80 N
Fn = Fg = 80 N
Identify m & g = 2 pts Fg = 8 × 10 = 2 pts Fg = 80 N = 2 pts Fn = Fg = 80 N = 2 pts
Q3 · Work done by a worker (8 pts)
Given: F = 20 N parallel to motion, d = 5 m. Formula: W = F · d
W = F · d = 20 × 5 = 100 J
Identify F & d = 2 pts Setup W = F · d, substitute 20 × 5 = 3 pts W = 100 J = 3 pts
Q4 · Kinetic energy (8 pts)
Given: m = 2 kg, v = 3 m/s. Formula: KE = ½ m v²
v² = 9 m²/s² → KE = ½ × 2 × 9 = 9 J
Identify m & v = 2 pts v² = 9 = 2 pts Substitute ½ × 2 × 9 = 2 pts KE = 9 J = 2 pts
Q5 · Momentum of a cart (8 pts)
Given: m = 3 kg, v = +4 m/s (right positive). Formula: p = m · v
p = m · v = 3 × (+4) = +12 kg·m/s (to the right)
Identify m & v = 2 pts Setup p = m · v, substitute 3 × 4 = 3 pts +12 kg·m/s with direction = 3 pts
Q6 · Impulse (6 pts)
Given: F = 5 N, Δt = 2 s. Formula: J = F · Δt
J = F · Δt = 5 × 2 = 10 N·s
Identify F & Δt = 2 pts Substitute 5 × 2 = 2 pts J = 10 N·s = 2 pts
Q7 · Recoil / conservation of momentum (4 pts)
Given: m₁ = 4 kg, m₂ = 2 kg, at rest, v₁f = −1 m/s. Formula: 0 = m₁·v₁f + m₂·v₂f
0 = 4 × (−1) + 2 · v₂f → 0 = −4 + 2·v₂f → v₂f = +2 m/s (to the right)
Setup 0 = m₁v₁f + m₂v₂f = 1 pt Substitute 4 × (−1) + 2·v₂f = 1 pt Solve −4 + 2·v₂f = 0 = 1 pt v₂f = +2 m/s with direction = 1 pt
Bonus · Braking time (3 pts — extra credit)
Given: m = 1000 kg, vi = +10 m/s, vf = 0, F = −200 N. Formula: J = F·Δt = Δp = m·vf − m·vi
Δp = (1000 × 0) − (1000 × 10) = −10000 kg·m/s
Δt = Δp / F = −10000 / −200 = 50 s
Identify values = 1 pt Δp = −10000 = 1 pt Δt = 50 s = 1 pt
